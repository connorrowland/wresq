William Ricigliano

Background/Education

William Ricigliano was born in Montclair, New Jersey on February 26, 1967. He graduated from the University of Massachusetts, Cum Laude in December of 1989 with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Political Science. Prior to entering law school, Mr. Ricigliano worked for Liberty Mutual Insurance Company in Saddle Brook, New Jersey as a Claims Adjuster in the Suit Unit. During his tenure at Liberty Mutual, Mr. Ricigliano supervised the litigation of various complex cases, including product liability claims, premises actions, and multi-vehicle automobile accidents. William Ricigliano New York Trial LawyerMr. Ricigliano graduated from Touro Law School located in Huntington, New York in 1994, in the top 20% of his class. Following his first year of law school, Mr. Ricigliano received the American Jurisprudence Award for Contracts I and Contracts II. As a result of his excellence in Contracts, Mr. Ricigliano served as a teaching assistant during his second year of law school. During that same year, he competed in the school’s moot court competition and earned a position on the Moot Court Board. He also served as President of the Columbian Lawyers Association, a group of law students and attorneys of Italian descent. During his third year of law school, Mr. Ricigliano competed in the William F. Starr Insurance Law Moot Court Competition in Hartford, Connecticut, and reached the quarterfinal round. He also earned a third American Jurisprudence Award in Trial Practice and prior to graduation, was awarded the Nassau-Suffolk Trial Lawyers Advocacy Award for his excellence in trial practice.

Legal Experience

Since 1999, Mr. Ricigliano has maintained his own law practice, dedicated to providing outstanding results to all the firm’s clients. Prior to starting his own practice, Mr. Ricigliano was trained as an associate in several well respected insurance defense firms in New York and New Jersey. The firm has offices located in Manhattan and Paterson, New Jersey and handles cases in the northern and central counties of New Jersey, the United States District Court for the District of New Jersey, the five boroughs of New York City, Westchester, Rockland, Nassau and Suffolk Counties and the Southern and Eastern Districts of New York. From 2001 to 2004, Mr. Ricigliano served as exclusive New York counsel for Security Indemnity Insurance Company, representing its insureds from premises and automobile liability claims. Mr. Ricigliano devotes his current practice to the litigation of catastrophic personal injury cases in New York and New Jersey, with an emphasis in trial practice and with a goal of improving community safety.

Publications

From 1998 to 1999, Mr. Ricigliano authored the monthly column It’s the Law for “Film Journal International,” a trade publication distributed to movie theater owners throughout the country. The column addressed various liability issues affecting the movie theater industry. Mr. Ricigliano has authored more than thirty articles regarding various legal issues and has been published in the National Law Journal, The New Jersey Law Journal, and “Box Office Magazine.” In November, 2002, his book, Premises Liability Claims: A Guide to Defending Owners, was published by the American Bar Association Publishing Division.

Frank Filopei

Background/Education

Frank Filopei was born in Bronx, New York on December 1, 1981. He graduated from Rutgers University in 2004 with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communications. Mr. Filopei graduated from Touro Law School located in Central Islip, New York in 2008.

Legal Experience

In 2007, Mr. Filopei began working at William Ricigliano, P.C. as a legal assistant. Upon his graduation in 2008, Mr. Filopei was hired as an associate with the firm. Mr. Filopei's practice focuses on premises liability claims, multi-vehicle motor vehicle collisions, environmental exposure torts, dram shop litigation, construction accidents and negligent hiring and assault cases. Mr. Filopei has developed a particular expertise in motion practice and heads the firm's trial preparation team.
